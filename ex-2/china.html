Government
China is a communist party-led state with a presidebt as cheif of state and a premier as head of government. The country's constitution was adopted on December 4,1982.
History
China is home to one of the world's oldest civilization, with a culture that stretches back almost 4000 years. The country's history is divided into dynasties, with the Qin dynasty beginning in 221 B.C. The last emperor was overthrown in 1912, and china became a republic. The People's Republic of China was established in 1949 after the communist party defeated the nationalist Kuomintang.
Economy
China is one of the world's fastest growing economics and a leading exporter. The country's economy is market-based, having changed from a centrally planned system in the 1970s.
